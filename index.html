Projects

Project 1

The first project is related to develop an application called CyHealth. CyHealth is a patient database system designed for three main user groups: system administrators, workers (doctors, nurses, and receptionists), and casual users (patients). System administrators manage worker and patient profiles, schedules, and data transfers. Workers include receptionists, who handle appointments and patient balances, and doctors/nurses, who access and update medical records, with doctors also fulfilling prescription refills. Patients can view their medical information, request appointments and prescription refills, and update non-medical details. All users must log in to access their respective home pages based on their role.
